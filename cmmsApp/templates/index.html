Containerised Solutions Process

Containerised Solutions Process

At iEngineering, we design and deliver robust, customised, and quick-to-deploy containerised solutions for industrial, infrastructure, and remote site applications. Our streamlined process ensures every unit is engineered to exact requirements, factory-built for quality, and delivered ready for immediate use‚Äîminimising site work, risks, and project timelines. From accommodation and site offices to electrical, control, and data infrastructure containers, we manage the complete lifecycle from concept to commissioning.

  1. Requirement Analysis & Concept Design

    We analyse operational, site, and compliance requirements to finalise architectural, structural, electrical, HVAC, and utility designs tailored to the application.

  2. Engineering, Fabrication & Quality Assurance

    Containers are engineered and factory-fabricated with complete fit-outs, insulation, services, and systems, followed by rigorous quality checks.

  3. Delivery & Site Positioning

    Finished units are transported and positioned using crane or lift-and-shift methods, ensuring safe placement and minimal site disruption.

  4. Final Hook-up, Testing & Handover

    Utilities are connected, systems tested, and the container is handed over fully operational and ready for immediate use.
